Definitions
Delinquent: Taxes that were not paid to the local treasurer. The County Treasurer adds a 4 percent administration fee and 1 percent per month interest. If not redeemed The taxes will be forfeited the March 1st.

Forfeited: The property is forfeited and a lien (COF) is recorded in the Register of Deeds against the taxes. The Wayne County Treasurer adds a $175 fee and $30 in recording fees and the interest is increased from 1 percent per month to 1.5 percent per month back to the date the taxes became delinquent. The parcel becomes subject to the transfer of ownership to the Wayne County Treasurer the following April 1st unless the forfeited taxes are redeemed.

Foreclosed: The ownership of the property has been transferred to Wayne County due to unpaid taxes and charges by judgment of the Circuit Court.

IRSPA: Delinquent/Forfeited taxes enrolled in IRSPA subject to reduced 0.5 percent per month interest rate, if not in default of the terms of the plan.

PAYSPA: Delinquent taxes enrolled in PAYS (Pay As You Stay) Program. No interest and fee accrue, if not in default of the terms of the plan.

Subject to Foreclosure: The property was Forfeited and is subject to Foreclosure and transfer of ownership to the Wayne County Treasurer at a future date due to outstanding delinquent taxes and charges. The Wayne County Treasurer has recorded a Lien (COF) in the Register of Deeds for the taxes due. The interest is increased from 1 percent per month to 1.5 percent per month back to the date the taxes became delinquent and all relevant statutory fees are added.

Homestead Denial An increase to the property taxes due to the denial of the property owner’s Principal Residence Exemption. Taxes will become delinquent on March 1st of the year following the increase.

State Bid: A lien for unpaid taxes and charges that were bid off in the name of the State of Michigan. Payment must be made in person and accompanied with a deed confirming ownership and, if located in the city of Detroit, a letter indicating that city taxes have been paid.

Unconfirmed: Same status as delinquent but not confirmed. This means that the local treasurer has not verified that the tax amount transferred to the Wayne County Treasurer is the actual tax amount that is delinquent. The status will be modified to delinquent once the tax amount is verified.
