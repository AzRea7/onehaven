Building Safety Division

Building Safety Division – Building Official Dennis Szymanski
City Hall
1st Floor
Building Safety Office
47450 Woodward Ave.
Pontiac, Michigan 48342
Telephone: 248-758-2800
Walk-In Service Hours: Monday through Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. 

The Building Safety Division administers and enforces the building code statutes and other related municipal ordinances (including the rental property code) which provide for minimum standards of building construction and use for the health and safety of the public.

NOTICE
Beginning June 1, 2024, Permits and Plan Reviews must be paid for up front when applying for a permit
 
ONLINE Permit Applications
Building permit applications are no longer accepted via email. However, homeowners and contractors can now apply for a permits online. Click here to apply: bsaonline.com

ONLINE search for Property Information
Online searches for property information can be made at: https://bsaonline.com/?uid=825
Search types include: permit lookups, inspections, violations, certificates and more...
Click here for instructions on how to use the website

ONLINE Building Department Payments
Building Department payments can be made online at: https://bsaonline.com/?uid=825
Click here for instructions on how to use the website

ONLINE Building Safety Document Retrieval
Document retrieval may be provided for Building and Planning Division documents that are not available online. To see if your document is available online please go to https://bsaonline.com/?uid=825.  If the document(s) you request are not available online, please complete and submit the Document Retrieval Application with the appropriate fee to the Building Safety Division. Once the application is received please allow 3-5 business days to process your request. You will be notified if/when documents are available for review. If your request is not handled through the Building Safety Division, please contact the appropriate department. If you have any questions, please contact the Building Safety Division at 248-758-2800.
Click here to download the Document Retrieval Application


All documents are formatted with the Adobe PDF format, and are best viewed using Acrobat 8.0 Click here to download Adobe to view forms.
