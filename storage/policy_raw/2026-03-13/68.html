  1. Assessment Department

    The Assessment Department is responsible for the valuing of all residential, commercial, industrial and personal property in the City of Livonia and answers inquiries from residents, property owners, mortgage companies, prospective buyers, businessmen, appraisers, and government agencies.

  2. Cable - Livonia Television

    Livonia Television features programs produced for the City by its own production staff and volunteers.

  3. City Clerk

    The City Clerk signs and records all ordinances and resolutions enacted or passed by City Council.

  4. Civil Service / Human Resources

    Oversees recruitment, examinations for employment and promotion, salary and wage administration, employee benefits, the application of rules and regulations.

  5. Communications

    The Communications Department is responsible for overseeing a wide variety of senior, youth and resident services.

  6. Economic Development

    Livonia is a big City with a small hometown charm and appeal. Whether you are starting a business, expanding your business, or need information about doing business in Livonia, this is the place to get started.

  7. Emergency Preparedness

  8. Engineering

    The Engineering Division inspects public construction work, reviews plans for public improvements, conducts engineering studies, prepares plans and specifications and administers contracts for the City's public improvement projects.

  9. Finance

    The Finance Department prepares and administers the general budget, keeps the City's accounts and financial records, and supervises purchases and expenditures.

  10. Fire Department

    Our family protects your family. We are professional firefighters and paramedics protecting homes, businesses, and lives in our community around the clock.

  11. Greenmead Historical Park

  12. Housing

    The mission of the Livonia Housing Commission is to provide adequate housing and community development assistance, and to expand and preserve decent, safe and affordable housing for low and moderate income families. The Commission is further committed to providing quality services to our clients in an effective, efficient and respectful manner.

  13. Information Systems

    The Information Systems (IS) Department is responsible for the computer hardware and software used by the City. The IS Department assists other City Departments in evaluating, selecting and implementing computer systems. It also sets the standards for City computer systems.

  14. Inspection (Building & Enforcement)

    Inspection examines and approves plans, issues permits and Certificates of Occupancy, conducts follow-up inspections to residential and commercial buildings.

  15. Law

    The mission of the City of Livonia Law Department is to provide excellent legal services to the City of Livonia, which includes the Mayor, City Council, City Commissions, and all City departments.

  16. Library

    The Livonia Public Library consists of four branches.

  17. Liv&Go

    The Livonia Transit program provides medical and non-medical transportation for the senior and disabled residents of Livonia.

  18. Ordinance

  19. Parks & Recreation

    Discover information about the Parks and Recreation Department events, classes, facilities, and more.

  20. Planning

    The Planning Department, in conjunction with the Planning Commission, has developed a master land use plan for Livonia.

  21. Police

    Get connected to the Livonia Police website.

  22. Public Works

    DPW maintains parks, roadways, water mains, and most of the storm and sanitary sewer main lines within the City.

  23. Sanitation

  24. Senior Wellness Center

  25. Treasurer's Office

    The Treasurer’s Department is responsible for collection, disbursement and custody of all revenues belonging to and held in trust by the City.
